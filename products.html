Real Results

Case Studies

Real deployments. Real data. Real impact — across agriculture, education, security, and enterprise in Nigeria.

🌾
Agriculture • Benue State
AgroBot Deployment on 200-Acre Rice Farm
Deployed AgroBot v2 across a 200-acre rice farm in Benue. The robot handled irrigation scheduling, pest detection alerts, and harvest readiness monitoring over a full growing season, replacing 6 manual workers for those tasks.
📈 Result: 34% increase in yield · 60% reduction in pesticide use · ₦4.2M operational savings in one season
🏫
Education • Lagos State
Smart School Rollout — 12-Campus Private School Network
Deployed Smart School Solutions across a 12-campus private school network in Lagos. Integrated attendance, fees, LMS, and parent communication into a single platform used by 8,000 students, 600 teachers, and 7,500 parents.
📈 Result: 40% reduction in admin workload · Fees collection rate up from 71% to 96% · Parent satisfaction score: 4.8/5
⛪
Faith-Based · Abuja FCT
Smart Church Platform — 15,000-Member Congregation
Implemented full Smart Church suite for a 15,000-member church in Abuja. Replaced paper-based systems with digital giving, automated member follow-up, and livestream management — cutting admin staff requirements by 40%.
📈 Result: Tithe income up 22% · 100% digital giving adoption in 3 months · 35 hours/week admin time saved
🚚
Logistics • Port Harcourt
Fleet Automation for Regional Distribution Company
Deployed our logistics optimisation system for a 50-truck distribution company in Port Harcourt. AI route planning reduced average delivery time and fuel consumption, while automated dispatch reduced dispatcher headcount by 3.
📈 Result: 23% fuel cost reduction · On-time delivery rate up from 68% to 94% · ₦7.8M annual savings
