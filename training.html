Recognition

Certifications & Pathways

Our certificates are recognized by industry partners and academic institutions across Nigeria. Choose your learning path below.

Learning Pathway

Foundation — Intro to AI & Robotics

Beginner-friendly 2-week course covering the history, concepts, and tools of AI and Robotics. No prior experience needed.

Core — Python, ML & Electronics

4–6 week deep dive into Python programming, machine learning algorithms, and basic electronics for robotics builds.

Advanced — Specialization Track

Choose your focus: Computer Vision, NLP, Autonomous Robots, or Embedded Systems. Practical project-based curriculum.

Capstone — Build & Present

Complete a real-world project, present to a panel of industry experts, and receive your Eagles AI Lab certificate.

🏅 Certificate Types

  • Certificate of Participation — Short Courses
  • Certificate of Completion — Bootcamps
  • Professional Certificate — Advanced Tracks
  • SIWES Completion Letter — Internship
  • STEM Facilitator Badge — School Program
