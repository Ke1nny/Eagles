Phase 01

Startup Incubation

Our flagship 6–12 month incubation program gives AI & Robotics startups everything they need to go from idea to investable business — workspace, infrastructure, coaching, and community.

01
Application & Selection
Submit your idea or MVP. Our panel of engineers and investors reviews 200+ applications each cohort. We accept the top 10–15 most promising teams.
Quarterly Intake
02
Workspace & Lab Access
Move into our Innovation Drive campus. Full access to robotics lab, GPU workstations, 3D printers, drone testing zone, and co-working spaces — all included.
On-Site
03
Build & Validate
Work alongside our research team to build, test, and refine your product. Weekly check-ins, user research support, and rapid prototyping resources.
6–12 Months
04
Demo Day & Investor Pitch
Present to an audience of 100+ investors, corporate partners, and media at our quarterly Demo Day. Many of our startups close their first round on the day.
Public Event
