AI Research

Artificial Intelligence

Deep learning architectures, language understanding, and computer vision — our AI research unit tackles the most complex challenges in machine intelligence.

🧠
Machine Learning
Deep learning architectures and neural networks solving complex pattern recognition and predictive modeling tasks. Our ML team works on supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning methods tailored to African datasets.
AI Research
💬
Natural Language Processing
Building language models that understand and generate African languages alongside global communication systems. Our NLP lab is developing the first large-scale Yoruba, Hausa, and Igbo language datasets and models.
AI Research
👁️
Computer Vision
Advanced image recognition and video analytics systems for security, agriculture, and medical diagnostics. Our models are trained on diverse African image data for real-world accuracy and reliability.
AI Research
📊
Predictive Analytics
Data-driven forecasting models for business intelligence, climate prediction, and public health systems. We build scalable pipelines that turn raw African data into actionable intelligence for governments and enterprises.
AI Research
Robotics

Robotics Research

From autonomous navigation to agricultural automation — our robotics lab builds intelligent machines that solve real problems on the ground.

🤖
Autonomous Robots
Self-navigating robotic systems with real-time decision making for industrial and commercial applications. Equipped with SLAM, LIDAR, and neural network inference on embedded hardware.
Robotics
🌾
Agricultural Robotics
Smart farming robots that automate planting, irrigation, pest detection, and harvest operations at scale. Our award-winning AgroBot prototype is currently in field trials across Benue and Anambra states.
Robotics
🚁
Drone Development
Custom UAV systems for surveillance, delivery logistics, mapping, and emergency response operations. Our SkyEagle drone features AI-guided navigation for last-mile delivery in underserved communities.
Robotics
⚙️
Embedded Systems
Low-power intelligent microcontroller systems for IoT devices and smart infrastructure integration. We design custom PCBs, firmware, and real-time OS configurations optimized for Nigeria's power environment.
Robotics
