Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to us directly and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

How do I enroll in an Eagles AI Lab training program? ▾
You can enroll by filling the contact form above, emailing training@eaglesailab.org, or visiting our Training page and clicking Register. Our admissions team will reach out within 48 hours to confirm your spot and provide payment details.
Do you offer online or hybrid training programs? ▾
Yes. Several of our programs — including AI for Youth and some short courses — are available in hybrid format (online + physical). Bootcamps and SIWES programs are primarily on-site at our Abuja campus, with some sessions streamed live.
How can my school partner with Eagles AI Lab for STEM programs? ▾
Schools can partner with us by contacting partnerships@eaglesailab.org or filling the form above with "STEM for Schools" as the subject. We'll schedule a meeting to discuss your school's needs, student numbers, and program options — many of which are subsidised for public schools.
What are the requirements to apply to the Innovation Hub incubation program? ▾
Applicants must have an early-stage AI or Robotics startup concept or MVP, be based in Nigeria or Africa, and be able to commit to the 6-month program. Teams of 2–5 people are preferred. Applications are reviewed quarterly — visit our Innovation Hub page to apply.
How can I donate or financially support Eagles AI Lab? ▾
We welcome donations from individuals, corporations, and foundations. You can donate via bank transfer, online payment, or equipment donation. Email donate@eaglesailab.org for our bank details and to discuss how your contribution will be used.
Are your training certificates recognised by employers and universities? ▾
Yes. Our certificates are co-signed by our partner universities and have been accepted by major employers including banks, fintechs, and tech companies across Nigeria. SIWES certificates follow the official ITF format and are accepted for university credit.
Can I visit the Eagles AI Lab campus for a tour? ▾
Absolutely! We welcome visitors, students, researchers, and potential partners. Book a facility tour by emailing info@eaglesailab.org or using the contact form above. Tours are available Monday–Friday between 10 AM – 4 PM by appointment.
