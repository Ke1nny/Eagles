Who We Are

About Eagles AI Lab

A research-driven institution committed to advancing artificial intelligence and robotics technology across Africa since 2020.

Purpose

Vision & Mission

🔭 Our Vision

To become Africa's leading hub for AI research, robotics development, and tech entrepreneurship — positioning Nigeria as a global player in frontier technology.

🎯 Our Mission

To democratize AI & Robotics education and drive technological innovation across Nigeria and Africa by making world-class training accessible, building homegrown solutions, and nurturing the next generation of tech leaders.

History

Our Story

From a small workshop in 2020 to Nigeria's premier AI & Robotics lab — driven by passion, purpose, and people.

2020
Founded in Abuja
Eagles AI Lab was founded by engineers and educators with a shared vision to advance AI and robotics in Nigeria.
2021
First Bootcamp
Launched our inaugural AI Bootcamp, training 80 students. AgroBot v1 was built by the inaugural cohort.
2022
STEM for Schools Launch
Expanded into secondary schools across FCT, Enugu, and Lagos, reaching thousands of students.
2023
Innovation Hub Opens
Launched the incubation program, welcoming the first cohort of AI & Robotics startups. 6 startups launched.
2024
National Recognition
Won the National Innovation Award for Agricultural Robotics. Published 5 peer-reviewed papers internationally.
2025
1,200+ Students Strong
Now training over 1,200 students, with partnerships across 10 universities, government agencies, and global tech companies.
